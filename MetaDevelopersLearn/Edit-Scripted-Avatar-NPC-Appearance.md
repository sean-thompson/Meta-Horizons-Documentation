# Edit Scripted Avatar NPC Appearance

[source](https://developers.meta.com/horizon-worlds/learn/documentation/desktop-editor/npcs/scripted-avatar-npcs/edit-scripted-avatar-npc-appearance)

You can change the appearance of your NPCs to create all kinds of characters in your game. Choose from a large palette of body types, hair and face characteristics, clothing, and accessories to define the specific look of your in-world characters.

## Edit Your Avatar Window

To modify your Scripted Avatar NPC’s appearance, select your Scripted Avatar NPC in the world. In the Properties panel, click **Edit Avatar**. The Edit Your Avatar window is displayed:

![Edit Your Avatar window](https://scontent.flba1-1.fna.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/469092523_603532498851372_6329087351078950612_n.png?_nc_cat=108&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=TuWNFx5o9kQQ7kNvwFIlVQY&_nc_oc=Adm4ePEX83CCzune2RiUka5LUK3T4ahengORr-4LOQWTxr6z-Q-2TLcN6YUEV9E_OWY&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent.flba1-1.fna&_nc_gid=R3Z2TWLeP_tdAj6uxAJzng&oh=00_AfSEJLPhVl0jXT-3ulfGwedaMbyy9FO_dPwfbi06ASS92g&oe=689BA214)

To change appearance:

*   Select a category on the left side of the screen.
    
    *   Some categories have sub-categories.
    
    *   Make your selection.
    
    *   If present, click the color wheel to change the applicable color.

*   When you have finished updating your NPC, click **Done editing**. Click **Save**.

*   Click **Done**.

*   Back in the Desktop Editor, verify that your Scripted Avatar NPC is selected. In the Properties panel, click the **Refresh button**.

*   Your Scripted Avatar NPC’s image is updated in the Properties panel. **Note**: You may have to Preview your world twice to see your Scripted Avatar NPC’s updated appearance. This is a known issue.
